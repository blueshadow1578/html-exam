Generation 1

in 2001 bill gates stood up on a podium and announced the ipod generation 1. It was revolutionary it had 32MB of on board ram, 5GB of ram which had never been added to a pocket device before, and allowed over 100 songs. it also had the ability to connect to your mac and download full albums at one time! the iPod Generation single handly brought apple from the gutters and straight to the leading music player company in the world quickly passing sony the now dethroned former king. The iPod classic was a piece of revolutionary music playing history. it was the first popular music player in the 20th century. it integrated a user-friendly interface and constantly changing technology like the gigabyte upgrades with each generation and the god tier ram (at the time) made a great experience for the user. they also created the best music integration process allowing all computers to integrate the music onto the iPod. the music quality was unprecedented you could hear the beat which would normally need high-quality speakers or high-quality headphones. the iPod dug apple out of the gutter and brought them to the spotlight. the iPod generation 1 was becoming the one and only way for people to listen to music. people bought it in mass, apple went from bankrupt to stars in just a few days and over time they kept growing changing becoming the behemoth that they are now they learned to control the industry and they do the same thing to this day.
